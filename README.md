# KeenKeeper

[Live Demo](https://keenkeeper-7.vercel.app)

**KeenKeeper** is a modern Personal Relationship Management (PRM) application designed to help you nurture and maintain meaningful connections. In a fast-paced world, KeenKeeper acts as your personal "shelf" for relationships, ensuring you stay in touch with the people who matter most through smart tracking, reminders, and interaction analytics.

## 🚀 Technologies Used

- **Framework:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router) & React 19
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS 4](https://tailwindcss.com/) & [DaisyUI 5](https://daisyui.com/)
- **Data Visualization:** [Recharts](https://recharts.org/) (Interactive Pie Charts)
- **Icons:** [React Icons](https://react-icons.github.io/react-icons/)
- **Notifications:** [React Toastify](https://fkhadra.github.io/react-toastify/)
- **State Management:** React Context API

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Smart Relationship Monitoring
Never let a connection fade away. KeenKeeper automatically categorizes your friends into **On Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** based on your custom interaction goals. The dashboard provides a high-level overview of who needs your attention and how many days have passed since your last contact.

### 2. Interactive Timeline & Activity Log
Keep a pulse on your social life with a comprehensive activity timeline. 
- **Log Interactions:** Easily record Calls, Texts, and Video Chats.
- **Filter & Sort:** Navigate through your history by interaction type or date (Newest/Oldest) to see exactly how you've been spending your time.

### 3. Visual Interaction Analytics
Gain data-driven insights into your communication patterns. The **Friendship Analytics** page uses dynamic charts to visualize your interaction distribution, helping you understand whether you're leaning more towards quick texts or meaningful video calls.

---

## 🛠️ Additional Functionalities
- **Quick Check-In:** Rapidly log new interactions directly from a friend's profile.
- **Flexible Management:** Snooze reminders for 2 weeks, archive old connections, or delete profiles as your social circle evolves.
- **Detailed Profiles:** Store bios, custom tags (e.g., "University", "Work", "Gym"), and specific relationship goals for every contact.
